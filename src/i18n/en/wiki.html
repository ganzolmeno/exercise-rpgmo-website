Wiki

In-game wiki

To enable wiki you need to enable Mods first. (Warning! Mods might not work on all devices)
  • Log in with your account at https://data.mo.ee
  • Menu -> Enable mods -> Yes
  • In "Mods info" -> Select All -> Load Selected
  • Click Wiki


Once enabled, mods let you see formulas, drops, level requirements and much more.
