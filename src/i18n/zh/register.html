Account


Create account
  • Open game https://data.mo.ee
  • Type in your account name
  • Type in the password you want
  • Click on
    Login/register
  • Play!


Lost password?
  • Open game https://data.mo.ee
  • Type in your account name
  • Type in random password(at least 6 symbols)
  • Click on
    Login/register
  • Click on
    [Reset]
  • Type in e-mail
  • Click on
    [Reset password]
  • Follow instructions, in case of problems write to support.


Transfer/unlock account from a provider
  • Log in with your account at the provider
  • From the top left Menu choose
    Player info
  • Fill in
    E-mail
    and press
    Save
  • Open up the
    Player info
    again.
  • Click on
    Reset password
  • You will receive an e-mail with a link
  • Follow instructions, in case of problems write to support.
  • Now you can log in at the provider and also use the official site with username and password.
